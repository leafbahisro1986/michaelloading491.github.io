The Performance of CORSAIR K55 RGB Gaming Keyboard

The CORSAIR K55 RGB gaming keyboard possesses a membrane design. This is where the keys are pushed down into a single plastic layer to keep track of button presses. This is in contrast to a mechanical keyboard, which has individual physical components in each button and has a contact mechanism that records button presses.

Membrane devices use fewer cheaper parts, so they are more affordable than mechanical devices. But these devices are always softer and slower than mechanical hardware. Although most companies will claim that their membrane keyboards duplicate mechanical hardware, they don’t have that speed.

The CORSAIR K55 RGB gaming keyboard does have one of the best membrane typing experiences on the market. CORSAIR’s buttons are fast and very satisfying. They are sturdy, and have a tactile design - so they experience noticeable bumps during typing, just like using a proper mechanical keyboard.
